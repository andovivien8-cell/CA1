Memory.Identity.Control

In these five films, memory is unreliable, identity is unstable, and truth is manipulated by forces both internal and external. From totalitarian surveillance to fractured minds and artificial environments, each story challenges the audience to question what is real. Reality Distorted: Memory, Identity & Control explores how cinema reveals the instability of perception — and the terrifying possibility that reality itself may be constructed.
